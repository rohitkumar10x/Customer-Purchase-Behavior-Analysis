## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer purchase behavior using Microsoft Excel.
The objective is to convert raw and messy data into meaningful insights that help businesses make data-driven decisions.

The dashboard highlights key patterns in customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and revenue distribution.

---

## 🎯 Key Insights

### 💰 Business Metrics

* Total Revenue: **$233K+**
* Total Customers: **7.6M**
* Average Purchase Value: **$59.8**

### 📊 Sales Insights

* Clothing & Accessories contribute **75%+ of total revenue**
* Footwear & Outerwear show potential growth opportunities

### 👥 Customer Insights

* Male customers generate higher revenue compared to females
* Age group **50+ is the top spending segment**

### 🔥 Customer Loyalty Insights

* Loyal customers contribute **majority of total revenue (~75%)**

### 💳 Purchase Behavior Insights

* Most customers purchase on a **monthly basis**
* Credit Card & digital payments are widely used
